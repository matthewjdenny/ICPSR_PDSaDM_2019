Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the  United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. REPORT ON ENTREPRENEURIAL IMPACT OF TECHNOLOGY TRANSFER.     (a) In General.--Not later than 180 days after the date of  enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General of the United States  shall submit to Congress a report on the entrepreneurial impact of  technology transfer at the National Laboratories (as the term is  defined in section 2 of the Energy Policy Act of 2005 (42 U.S.C.  15801)).     (b) Basis of Report.--The report under subsection (a) shall be  based on an evaluation of quantitative performance metrics, including--             (1) the number of licenses granted to small businesses;             (2) the number of start-up businesses created;             (3) the number of cooperative research and development          agreements and collaborations involving small businesses and          the total number of businesses involved in those agreements and          collaborations;             (4) the period of time required for execution of a license;          and             (5) the number of jobs created.                                  &lt;all&gt;
